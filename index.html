Signal Rules

  • BUY: Price above SMA 50, near EMA 9, RSI 45–65, and bouncing.
  • WAIT: Trend okay but price extended, RSI too high, or setup not clean.
  • SELL: Price below EMA 9 with weakness, RSI <40, or below SMA 50.
